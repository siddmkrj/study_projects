Introduction to Python Project : FoodHub Data Analysis

Problem Statement

The number of restaurants in New York is growing, and many students and professionals rely on online food delivery services due to their busy lifestyles. FoodHub, a food aggregator, connects customers with multiple restaurants through a mobile app, handling order placement, restaurant confirmation, and delivery.

FoodHub has collected data on customer orders and wants to analyze it to understand restaurant demand and improve customer experience. As a Data Scientist, my task is to analyze the provided dataset, answer key business questions, and derive insights to enhance FoodHubs operations and profitability.

Data Dictionary

order_id Unique ID of the order.

customer_id ID of the customer who placed the order.

restaurant_name Name of the restaurant from which the order was placed.

cuisine_type Type of cuisine ordered by the customer.

cost_of_the_order Price paid for the order.

day_of_the_week Indicates whether the order was placed on a weekday (Monday to Friday) or weekend (Saturday and Sunday).

rating Customer rating for the order (out of 5).

food_preparation_time Time (in minutes) taken by the restaurant to prepare the food.

delivery_time Time (in minutes) taken by the delivery person to deliver the food to the customer.
